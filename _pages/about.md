I am a Lecturer / Assistant Professor (Data Science Discipline) at the School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, The University of Queensland (UQ), Australia. My research interests include domain adaptation, domain generalization, few-shot / zero-shot learning, active learning. I was the recipient of the Google PhD Fellowship and ICT Young Achiever, WiT. I received my PhD in Computer Science from UQ in 2021, advised by Prof Helen Huang and Dr Mahsa Baktahmotlagh. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Yingcai Honors College, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2017.
